Six Tips to Improve your Productivity

Productivity is related to timing. You can't call yourself productive if you spend three hours completing a 30-minute task. That’s why you need to find a timing system that can help you become more efficient.

Some experts suggest using the 15-minute rule. Divide your time into 15-minute intervals and time yourself. This way, if the task is quick, you’ll only spend 15 minutes on it. If the job is long and complicated, you'll get it done step-by-step. Timing the process urges you to complete the task as soon as possible without wasting time. Also, keeping track of time will make you more aware of how fast minutes pass.

Try to turn off your phone when you're working or put it on silence mode. Alternatively, you can download an app that mutes specific applications such as social media, while notifying when you get a new email. Putting your phone away can also work or setting a custom sound for each application.
