Our Classes

Discover new training techniques and exercises through a variety of fitness classes that offer a dynamic and efficient full-body workout.

Crossfit Level 1

One weight is all it takes. Delete distraction and zero in on results with a next-generation weight training class that strips your training to the core. Work with a single weight and ignite more muscles per move for a total body workout.

BootCamp

This is athletic training, redefined. Boost your performance with the same equipment used by professional athletes. Compete in rapid-fire drills, then unleash your power with a burst of club strikes. This is how champions are made.

Energy Blast

Blaze through a day's worth of cardio in thirty flat. Burn through explosive drills in a high-intensity challenge that demands everything you've got and incinerates your goals before you know it.

CLASSIC BODY BALANCE

Get swept off your feet in a burst of dance-inspired cardio. Train like a dancer with a fierce athletic edge as you use light round-weights to boost your burn. Twist and turn to simple choreography in a music-driven flow that'll have your heart racing and your spirits energized.
