About Me

Passion for Photography

I started taking pictures in my Canon EOS 2800 as a hobby back in 2008. Soon enough, I started spending a lot of time behind the lens and gradually started taking pictures as a commercial pursuit. Throughout the years, I've become adept at handling all types of camera frames and lenses, to the point where I can quickly determine the best camera to use for a specific picture. I've taken classes as well, learning about the more advanced aspect like aperture and focus, but in the end, I believe that what sets me apart is my knack for taking perfect pictures at the right moment.
